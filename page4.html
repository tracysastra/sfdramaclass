M A M M A   M I A

Mamma mia tells the tale of a hotelier planning her daughter's wedding with the help of her two friends as her daughter secretly invites 3 men from her mother's past in hopes of finding her real father to have him escort her down the isle on her wedding day.

S O U N D   O F   M U S I C

Sound of music is a musical drama based off a true story of the Von Trapp family singers, one of the world's best-known concert groups in the era immediately preceding World War II. Sound of music features a postulant who becomes a governess to a widowed captain's seven children and brings life into their house by music.
