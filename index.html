WHY JOIN DRAMA CLASS?

  1. IMPROVE YOUR CONFIDENCE - Drama is the best elective to take if you're looking to improve your confidence. Students who are extremely shy at first grow to become confident by the end of the year after participating in drama. As you'll be doing many performances, whether it be in class or assembly, you'll learn to ignore your anxiety and increase your self-esteem.
  2. BE ACTIVE - In drama class, you'll be really active. How great would it be if you get to be active and enjoy the class with your peers? All your other classes require you to write and write, and that's it. In drama class, you actually get up and move around, communicating with your friends and teacher.
  3. EXPRESS YOUR CREATIVITY - This class is the best opportunity for you to express your creativity. Other classes make you read books and write things you don't have a say in. On the other hand, drama will make you write scripts, design costumes, props and act. You'll have the creative freedom not given by other classes. It's time to unleash your creative side!
  4. IT'S FUN - Need we say more? You'll be bonding and rehearsing with your friends. What's funner than hanging out with your friends?
What are you waiting for? Join drama class now!
